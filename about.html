About Us

The Association of Chairmen of Local Government Service Commissions in Nigeria (ACLGSCN) was established in 2005 to create a unifying platform for the leadership of all state Local Government Service Commissions (LGSCs) and the FCT. It advocates for best practices in governance, professionalism in the local government workforce, and collaborative development policies across Nigeria.

Our Vision

To be the leading catalyst for transformative local government administration and workforce development in Nigeria.

Our Mission

  • To promote effective and accountable local governance.
  • To ensure professional development and welfare of local government staff.
  • To advocate for policy reforms and capacity building at the grassroots.
  • To serve as a bridge between federal/state governments and local councils.

Core Values

  • Transparency and Accountability
  • Equity and Fairness in Recruitment & Promotion
  • Service Excellence
  • Inclusiveness and Collaboration

Strategic Objectives

  • Strengthening the LGSC institutional framework
  • Encouraging nationwide standards in civil service ethics
  • Hosting national summits, workshops, and forums
  • Digital transformation of LG personnel records
