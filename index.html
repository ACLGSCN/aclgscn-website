With representation from all 36 states and the Federal Capital Territory, we are committed to advancing grassroots governance, public service excellence, and national development.

Overview of the Local Government Service Commission System in Nigeria

The Local Government Service Commission (LGSC) in Nigeria is a statutory body established in each state, responsible for the management and administration of local government personnel. Its core mandates include the recruitment, promotion, discipline, and transfer of staff, as well as the formulation of uniform guidelines to ensure a professional and effective local government workforce. The LGSC ensures that standards are maintained across all local councils, fostering accountability and service delivery at the grassroots level.

  • Personnel management for over 150,000 local government employees nationwide
  • Ensuring merit-based recruitment and promotions
  • Standardizing operational procedures across all local governments
  • Supporting the professional development of staff
  • Promoting transparency, accountability, and good governance at the local level
